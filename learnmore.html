Computer Engineer

Daily Life and Money

The “typical day” things that Computer Hardware Engineers do, they update knowledge and skills to keep up with rapid advancements in computer technology. They might also provide technical support to designers, marketing and sales departments, suppliers, engineers and other team members throughout the product development and implementation process. The average for a computer engineer is $112,760 per year. Overall, the average pay for a hardware engineer is 5% higher than for a software engineer.
